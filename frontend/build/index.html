04 · How It Works

2 Chambers keep air flowing.

Disposable bags force everything through the same space — that's why they clog. Muk Buddy's 2-chamber design separates the two jobs:

★ FIG.01 · HOW IT WORKS ★
★ PATENT-PENDING 2-CHAMBERMB—01

* Diagram for illustration. Real product photo replaces this.

  1. 01

    Air + Debris Enters

    Dirty air, dust, and liquid get pulled into Muk Buddy through the vacuum's intake — same as always.

  2. 02

    Debris Drops Out

    The inner column redirects heavy material downward. Dust, water, and solids fall into the bottom debris chamber — out of the airflow path.

  3. 03

    Clean Air Rises

    Filter-grade reusable fabric lets air pass through freely while trapping fine particles. The airflow column stays open.

  4. 04

    Filter Stays Dry

    On wet jobs, liquid collects at the bottom chamber. The fabric breathes — so your vacuum's filter never sees the moisture.

  5. 05

    Motor Is Protected

    Clean, consistent airflow reaches the motor. No choking. No overheating. No early burnout.

  6. The Outcome

    Suction stays strong. Filter stays open. Motor keeps breathing — wet or dry.
